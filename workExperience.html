CNC Wire cutter

Operational CNC Wire Cutter

This machine, now in operation at Zaber Technologies, uses pneumatically controlled 5C collet chucks to pull in wire from the right side. An 8-inch abrasive cutting wheel, driven from behind, cuts the wire while it rotates. This controlled rotation ensures that any minimal burrs form at the center of the wire rather than along an edge.

Work in progress

The machine uses pneumatics to controll the 5C collet chucks, stepper motors to control the postion of the sub spindle, Cutter spindle, and rotation of the main and sub spindles. A Teknic motor is used to drive the abrasive cutting wheel.
